Walk Through

Welcome to the KAOS Team!

This resource is packed full of useful information for you as a new KAOS Clinician. Swipe through to orientate yourself, or skip it - you are a clinician after all and are pretty good at picking stuff up as you go along...

Hannah, Simon and Barney lead the service on the day to day. As a member of the team though you will be supporting Barney with any questions he has when he goes out to meet the young people on the wards.

Most advice you give will be by phone. Try and touch base with him at least twice a day to discuss cases.

Locked behind the 'hamburger' icon is a list of information for young people to browse about King's, about their stay as well as signposts to help for questions about their health.

The rota will tell you who is on call on a given day, as well as a snapshot of the week ahead. Your days will show up on the calendar as blue dots. If you want to swap a shift, select the date you want to do and slide right to reveal the swap icon. This will send the person rostered a request.

That's it!
