About

What is KAOS?

KAOS stands for King's Adolescent Outreach Service

Who Are We?

A team dedicated to looking after patients in the 16-19 age range. We have specialists of all different kinds: doctors, nurses, play-specialists, therapists and youth workers.

Why?

We realise that being in hospital is not fun for anyone, especially young people. We realised people feel out of place in between paediatric and adult care - we are here to bridge that gap.

How can you find us?

No need! We will come and find you during your stay. If you want to make contact ask your nurse to give us a call or find our contacts on the contacts page.
