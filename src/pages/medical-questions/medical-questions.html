Medical Questions

Our bodies, health conditions and procedures are all really complex. It’s completely normal to have lots of questions, so don’t hold back! It’s good to ask questions about your health and be involved with your care. In first instance, always ask one of your medical team if you don’t understand something and if they can’t help you, ask them who might be able to talk to you. There are also useful digital resources – try the NHS go app (include link https://nhsgo.uk/ ), a free health app designed specifically for young people or the NHS A-Z of health conditions and treatments at https://www.nhs.uk/Conditions/Pages/hub.aspx . Be aware that some online health sites and forums are less reliable and, in some cases, might not helpful or accurate. Always ask your medical team on the ward if you are worried or have questions.
