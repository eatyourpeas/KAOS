Internet and Wifi Access
3G and 4G across King's College Hospital is variable. Wifi access for patients and members of the general public is available at the following prices:
1 day £4.50 for 1 device
£6.75 for 2 devices
£9.00 for 3 devices
 1 week £12.50 for 1 device
£18.75 for 2 devices
£25.00 for 3 devices
 1 month £17.50 for 1 device
£26.25 for 2 devices
£35.00 for 3 devices
To access it, turn on your WIFI, select WifiSpark and follow the on-screen instructions.
